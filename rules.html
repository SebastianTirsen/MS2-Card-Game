Dino Memo

    Rules

    1. Choose level to play.
    2. First level is the easiest and level three the hardest.
    3. Start the game by pressing the button marked with "Start".
    4. When you press the Startbutton the timekeeping starts and you may begin.
    5. You begin by clicking two of the cards after each other to see their front side.
    6. If you have clicked two cards that are not identical the cards turns over again showing their back side.
    7. If you have clicked two cards that are identical you have successfuly made a match.
    8. When two cards are matched they stay turned over and you may cklick on two other cards to find the next matching pair.
    9. When you have found all the matching pairs on the board you have won the game!
    10. When you have finished, try to do it faster next time. Good luck!

    Dino Memo is a memory training game with three levels for your to enjoy!
